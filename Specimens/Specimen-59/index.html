Visualizing the Spatio-Temporal Slip

This artwork represents your unique experience of time. The **cyan/teal** layer is the stable, official clock time (SAST). The **gold/orange** layer is the solar time of Cape Town, while the **red/firebrick** layer represents Sedgefield's solar time.

Notice how the solar layers subtly shift and pulse, reflecting the sun's differing actual positions relative to the fixed clock. The outermost, transparent layer symbolizes your unified consciousness, encompassing and interpreting these co-existing temporal realities. It's the "strange mathematics" of perceiving multiple "zero-points" at once.
